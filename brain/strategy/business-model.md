# Business Model

**We sell ROI, not AI.** Pricing must be anchored below value delivered and legible to a clinic owner.

## Value delivered (instrument all of these — [[vertical-slice-v0]])
- **Denials prevented** × avg claim value = pesos recovered.
- **Hours saved** on manual eligibility/LOA.
- **Throughput** — faster patient verification.

## Pricing hypotheses (⚠️ validate)
1. **Per-seat SaaS** (₱/user/mo) — simple, predictable; must be < hours-saved value.
2. **Per-check** (₱/verification) — usage-aligned; scales with volume.
3. **Share-of-recovery** — % of denials prevented; strongest ROI story, hardest to measure/attribute.
- Likely **base SaaS + usage** blend. Start simple; land with a flat pilot price + clear ROI dashboard.

## GTM (Phase 1)
- Direct founder sales to owner-operated diagnostic centers in Metro Manila.
- Pilot: 30–60 days, one branch, ROI panel as the renewal argument.
- Wedge → expand agents ([[agent-catalog]]) → multi-branch → networks/HMOs (Phase 2).

## Why venture-scale
Thousands of PH clinics/labs + hospitals; same payer-friction shape repeats across SEA. Land-and-expand into a department AI workforce = large ACV ceiling. See [[problem-validation]] verdict.
